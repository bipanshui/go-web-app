Best ways to connect

  • Members-only Slack
    Ask questions, share progress, and get help from the community.
  • YouTube playlists
    Use the tutorial links on the courses page to follow along at your own pace.
  • Course feedback
    If you spot a gap or want a topic covered, mention it when you reach out.

What to include

When you ask for help, include the course name, the exact step you reached, and the error message or output you are seeing. Clear context usually gets you a faster answer.

  • State the topic or playlist you are following.
  • Share the command, screenshot, or error text.
  • Explain what you expected to happen.
