About the author

Practical DevOps teaching, shaped by real-world engineering work.

Abhishek builds and shares content with a focus on helping people understand the ecosystem, not just the syntax. The goal is to make the learning path approachable, useful, and grounded in the workflows teams actually use.

Why this content exists

Learning DevOps can feel fragmented. There are too many tools, too many opinions, and too little structure for someone starting out. This site keeps the learning path simple: understand the fundamentals, practice the tools, and then combine them into working systems.

The courses are built around free playlists, hands-on demos, and repeatable patterns so you can build confidence without being overwhelmed.

Focus areas

  • Open source collaboration and community contributions.
  • GitOps, Kubernetes, cloud platforms, and infrastructure automation.
  • Clear explanations that reduce friction for beginners.

Community work

Abhishek has contributed to open source projects such as Argo CD, Argo Rollouts Manager, GitOps Operator, and related ecosystem projects. The teaching style on this site reflects that background: practical, opinionated where it matters, and focused on what helps you ship better systems.

If you want the most direct learning path, head to the courses page and follow the curated playlists in order.
