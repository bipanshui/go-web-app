What you get here

DevOps is easier to learn when the journey is broken into small, useful pieces. The content on this site is organized to help you understand why each tool matters before you start memorizing commands.

You will find the main ideas behind automation, deployment, infrastructure, and cloud operations, along with recommended playlists to keep your momentum high.

  • Begin with DevOps fundamentals and the delivery lifecycle.
  • Move into CI/CD, infrastructure as code, and cloud services.
  • Use playlists that map directly to hands-on practice.
